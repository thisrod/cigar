\title Anushka's trap

The goal is to solve a Gross-Pitaevskii equation
$${∂ψ\over ∂t} = -i{ℏ\over m}\left(-{∇²\over 2}+{x²+y²\over 2l₀⁴}+½{z²\over L⁴}
	+4πa|ψ|²\right)ψ.
$$

The gas is~$10⁵$ atoms of~${}^{87}\rm Rb$, with~$ℏ/m=ℏ/87\,\rm
u=7.300×10^{-10}\,m²/s$.  The tight trap length is~$l₀=√{ℏ/(m·2π×100\,\rm
Hz)}=1.078×10^{-6}\,\rm m$.  The loose trap length
is~$L=√{ℏ/(m·2π×11.96\,{\rm Hz})}=3.116×10^{-6}\,{\rm m}=2.892\,l₀$,
and~$L⁴=(100/11.96)²·l₀⁴=69.91\,l₀⁴$.  The repulsion constant
is~$g=4πa=4π×100.4×a₀=0.06194\,l₀.$
